# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to a respectful, professional, and inclusive project environment.

## Expected Behavior

- Use clear and constructive communication.
- Focus critique on technical decisions and evidence.
- Be respectful of different experience levels and backgrounds.
- Help contributors understand project standards and tradeoffs.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Deliberately derailing technical discussion.
- Reporting security issues publicly before maintainers have a chance to fix them.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments, close discussions, or restrict participation when behavior harms the project community.

Security issues should follow [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).
